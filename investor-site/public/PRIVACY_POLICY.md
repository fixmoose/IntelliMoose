## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Dejan Obradovic ("Founder," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you access the IntelliMoose Investor Portal (the "Site"). This Site is intended for private distribution to potential investors, advisors, and business partners only.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

When you interact with the Site or contact us regarding investment opportunities, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
- **Professional Information:** Company name, job title, LinkedIn profile
- **Investment Information:** Accredited investor status, investment capacity, areas of interest
- **Communications:** Content of emails, messages, or calendar invitations sent to us

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect:

- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, time spent on site, links clicked, documents downloaded
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
- **Referral Data:** Source that referred you to the Site (e.g., email link, direct navigation)
- **Analytics Data:** Via third-party services such as Vercel Analytics (if enabled)

### 3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

- **Mutual connections:** Introductions from advisors, investors, or colleagues
- **Publicly available sources:** LinkedIn, Crunchbase, AngelList, company websites
- **Background checks:** For accredited investor verification (with your consent)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Evaluate investment opportunities:** Assess your suitability as an investor and respond to inquiries
2. **Communicate with you:** Send updates, pitch materials, legal documents, and meeting invitations
3. **Improve the Site:** Understand how visitors interact with content to improve user experience
4. **Comply with legal obligations:** Maintain records for tax, securities law, and corporate governance purposes
5. **Protect our rights:** Prevent fraud, enforce terms of use, and protect intellectual property

### 2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

- In response to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process
- To comply with securities regulations (e.g., SEC filings, Form D)
- To protect our legal rights or the rights of others

### 3. Business Transfers

If IntelliMoose is acquired, merged, or sells assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. You will be notified of any such change.

### 4. Investor Syndicate or Fund

If you invest through a syndicate, fund, or special purpose vehicle (SPV), your information may be shared with the lead investor or fund manager.

### 5. With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties if you explicitly consent (e.g., introductions to co-investors or advisors).
