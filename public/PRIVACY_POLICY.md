# Privacy Policy

**IntelliMoose Investor Portal**

Effective Date: December 22, 2025

---

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how IntelliMoose ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information obtained through our investor portal website.

**This portal is intended solely for accredited investors and qualified parties reviewing potential investment opportunities.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Professional Information**: Company affiliation, title, professional background
- **Investor Status**: Accredited investor status verification documents
- **Communications**: Correspondence, inquiries, and feedback

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, time spent, click patterns
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics or similar services (anonymized)

### 3. Documents and Materials
- Access logs for downloaded pitch decks and materials
- Viewing history of confidential documents

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

### Investment Process
- Verifying accredited investor status
- Providing access to confidential investment materials
- Responding to inquiries about the investment opportunity
- Facilitating due diligence discussions

### Communications
- Sending updates about the company and fundraising process
- Responding to questions and requests
- Providing customer support

### Analytics and Improvement
- Understanding how visitors use our portal
- Improving website functionality and content
- Analyzing investor interest and engagement

### Legal and Compliance
- Complying with securities laws and regulations
- Maintaining records required by law
- Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access

---

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

### With Service Providers
- **Hosting providers** (for website operation)
- **Email services** (for communications)
- **Analytics providers** (for usage insights, anonymized)
- **Legal counsel** (for compliance and legal matters)

All service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

### With Co-Investors and Advisors
- Information may be shared with co-investors or advisors participating in the funding round
- Sharing is limited to information necessary for investment evaluation

### For Legal Reasons
- To comply with legal obligations, subpoenas, or court orders
- To enforce our terms of use or protect our rights
- To prevent fraud or investigate suspected illegal activity
- To protect the safety and security of individuals

### In Business Transfers
- If IntelliMoose is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred
- You will be notified of any such change in ownership or control

---

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

### Technical Safeguards
- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Secure hosting infrastructure
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security updates and patches

### Organizational Safeguards
- Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
- Confidentiality agreements with employees and contractors
- Regular security training and awareness

**However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.**

---

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements (typically 7 years for investor records)
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell information)
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Contact us at privacy@intellimoose.ai to exercise these rights.

---

## European Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Right to withdraw consent
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

**Legal Basis for Processing**: We process your information based on:
- Legitimate interests (evaluating potential investors)
- Contractual necessity (facilitating investment process)
- Legal compliance (securities regulations)
- Consent (where applicable)
